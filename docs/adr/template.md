## Context

What forces are at play? What constraints, prior decisions, or operational
realities make this decision necessary? Keep to what's needed to follow the
decision — don't write a history paper.

## Decision

What we're going to do, stated plainly. Should be unambiguous enough that a
reader can tell whether a future implementation honors or departs from it.

## Consequences

**Positive**: what gets easier or unlocked.
**Negative**: what gets harder or accepts cost.
**Neutral**: what gets reshaped without net benefit/loss.

## Alternatives considered

The serious options we evaluated and rejected, with reasons. One paragraph
each is usually enough.

## Open items deferred

Things this ADR explicitly does not decide. Pointers forward to where they'll
be addressed.
